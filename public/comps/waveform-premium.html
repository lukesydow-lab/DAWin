Waveform Premium · Gradient + Glow

A single style with a vertical gradient body and a soft owner-color halo. Strips itself back to clean precision at microscopic zoom.
The premium style layers four passes on a single canvas: outer glow halo → mid bloom → vertical gradient body → bright peak stroke. Each pass is owner-color tinted at different opacities. The effect reads as soft and expensive at distance, sharp and surgical up close. At microscopic zoom all glow/gradient passes drop away — just dots and a thin line.
